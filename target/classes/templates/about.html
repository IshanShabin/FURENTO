About FURENTO

We are on a mission to make premium furniture accessible to everyone.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, FURENTO started with a simple idea: why buy expensive furniture when you can rent it for a fraction of the cost? We realized that modern living requires flexibility, and traditional furniture ownership creates unnecessary burdens.

Today, we offer a curated collection of high-quality furniture for homes and offices. Whether you're moving to a new city, setting up a temporary workspace, or just love changing your decor often, FURENTO is here to help you live better.

Our Values

Customer First

We obsess over customer experience, from easy ordering to free maintenance and returns.

Sustainability

Renting extends the lifecycle of furniture, reducing waste and our environmental footprint.

Quality

We only offer furniture that meets rigorous standards for durability, comfort, and style.
